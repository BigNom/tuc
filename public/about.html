About the Ultimate Clean

The Ultimate Clean is a family based Perth Carpet Cleaning company.

It was established in 2009 to meet the growing need for high quality, affordable carpet, tile and upholstery cleaning for residential and commercial properties in Perth. Since then it has established itself as a authority in the cleaning industry.

The Ultimate Clean also works with a number of experts in their own industries such as carpet re-dying companies, marketing consultants, and Social media marketing professionals, helping them to deliver superior results to their clients.

Director and owner/operator Brett Prosser has the highest level training in the industry. Certificate lll (CPP 31111) and is the current President of the Carpet Cleaning Association of WA. Training to get certificate lll involves a two week full time course outlining fibre construction, basic chemistry, advanced stain removal, leather, health and safety training... etc).

Brett Prosser has the passion and experience to deliver the most thorough clean to every customer at a very competitive price. The Ultimate Clean focuses on cleaning for health to help improve the indoor air quality of every property we clean, because we know that we make a difference.

Our Vision

The Ultimate Clean deliver floor cleaning that reflects the way we do business: professional, efficient, simple, and producing exceptional results. We do NOT believe in making a client fit a particular box or system, we tailor make the box to fit the client. We believe in the ethical conduct of our business and expect our clients to have similar values.
